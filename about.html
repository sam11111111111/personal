About Me

Hi, I’m Samuel, a passionate developer with a love for AI applications, coding, and building tools that make a positive impact. My journey in technology began long before I ever wrote a line of code. Growing up, I was always captivated by the power of computers, especially the endless possibilities that technology could offer to solve real-world problems. I remember being fascinated by how machines worked, from simple calculators to complex computers, and would spend hours dismantling and reassembling old gadgets just to understand their inner workings.

Early Life and Education

Childhood and family

I was born on Apr 2, 2001, in Bishoftu, Ethiopia, and i grow up with my family. My mother,Birke Garedew, was a model and dietitian born in Saskatchewan, Canada, and raised in Ethiopia. My father, Agide Assefa , was an electromechanical engineer, pilot, sailor, and property developer. I also have a younger brother, Yohans, and a younger sister, Nardos .

I attended Bethelhem High School, where not that much near to my home, I always had a deep love for reading, which played a significant role in shaping who I am today. Some of the books that inspired me were The Lord of the Rings, The Foundation series, and The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy.

I've always loved playing video games since childhood, and by the age of 16, I started thinking about developing and designing them. At 17, I began learning programming languages like Python, C, C++, C#, Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I started with simple code, like adding two numbers, and eventually created SAYO, a local multiplayer game.

In addition to game development, I'm also interested in cybersecurity and work with Linux distributions like Kali and Parrot. I’m fortunate to have the support of my family as I continue to explore these passions.

These early experiences—my family background and my self-driven pursuit of knowledge—were crucial in shaping my future path in technology and entrepreneurship.

Career and Projects

After college, I embarked on projects that combined my technical skills with my desire to make a positive impact. One of my most ambitious undertakings has been Ask Dr. Bot, a medical chatbot designed to improve healthcare access in Ethiopia. The bot aims to provide users with reliable health information, particularly for those who may not have immediate access to medical professionals. This project has been a rewarding challenge, involving the development of a responsive user interface, accurate and culturally sensitive information processing, and ethical data management.

In addition to Ask Dr. Bot, I’m working on a portable online library, an initiative aimed at making educational resources available to anyone, anywhere. This project aligns with my belief in the power of knowledge-sharing to drive social change and individual growth. The library includes curated resources and interactive features that promote a more engaging learning experience.

Current Work and Vision

Currently, I am focused on several exciting projects, including using TensorFlow and YOLO for object detection, and developing a cooking guide to help people explore new recipes and cooking techniques. Additionally, I am working on creating a digital Ethiopia, which will include tools like an interactive map and an online price-checking system to improve access to information. My vision is to continue leveraging AI and machine learning to solve real-world challenges, with a strong focus on practical applications that can benefit communities. I aim to create solutions that are accessible, sustainable, and impactful, all while driving innovation in fields like AI, technology, and digital services.
